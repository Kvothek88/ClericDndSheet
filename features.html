FEATURES & TRAITS

CLASS FEATURES

Name
Description
Spellcasting

You can cast prepared cleric spells using WIS as your spellcasting modifier (Spell DC 17, Spell Attack +9) and cast them as rituals if they have the ritual tag. You can use a holy symbol as a spellcasting focus.

Divine Domain

You choose a divine domain that grants you additional spells and other features related to your deity.Twilight Domain

Domain Spells

You gain domain spells at the cleric levels listed in the Twilight Domain Spells table.

Bonus Proficiencies

You gain proficiency with martial weapons and heavy armor.

Eyes of Night

You have darkvision out to a range of 300 feet. As an action, you can share this darkvision with up to 5 willing creatures within 10 ft. of you for 1 hour. Once used, you can’t share it again until you finish a long rest—or you expend a spell slot of any level to do so again.

Eyes of Night: 1 Action / Long Rest

Vigilant Blessing

As an action, you give one creature you touch (including yourself) advantage on its next initiative roll. This benefit ends immediately after the roll or if you use this feature again.

Vigilant Blessing: 1 Action

Channel Divinity

You can channel divine energy to fuel magical effects a number of times per short rest.

Channel Divinity: (No Action) | Uses: / Short Rest

Channel Divinity: Turn Undead: 1 Action

TCoE, pg. 35 — Channel Divinity: Twilight Sanctuary

As an action, you present your holy symbol and a sphere of twilight appears (30‑ft radius, lasts 1 minute or until you are incapacitated). When a creature ends its turn in the sphere, you may grant it either 1d6+9 temporary hit points or end one effect causing it to be charmed or frightened.

Channel Divinity: Twilight Sanctuary: 1 Action

Destroy Undead

When an undead fails its saving throw against your Turn Undead feature, it is instantly destroyed if its CR is lower than the threshold for your level.

Destroy Undead: (No Action)

Steps of Night

As a bonus action in dim light or darkness, you can give yourself a flying speed equal to your walking speed for 1 minute. You can do this 4 times, regaining all uses after a long rest.

Steps of Night: 1 Bonus Action | Uses: / Long Rest

Divine Strike

Once on each of your turns, when you hit with a weapon attack, you can deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage.

Divine Strike: (No Action)

SPECIAL TRAITS

Ability Score Increase

Your Constitution score increases by 2.

Darkvision

You can see in darkness (shades of gray) up to 60 ft.

Dwarven Resilience

You have advantage on saving throws against poison and resistance against poison damage.

Dwarven Combat Training

You gain proficiency with the battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, and warhammer.

Tool Proficiency

You gain proficiency with your choice of smith’s tools, brewer’s supplies, or mason’s tools. Brewer's Supplies

Stonecunning

When making an Intelligence (History) check related to stonework, you are considered proficient and add double your proficiency bonus.

Ability Score Increase

Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

Dwarven Toughness

Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and increases by 1 each time you gain a level.
